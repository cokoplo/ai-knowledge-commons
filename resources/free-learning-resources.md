# Free Learning Resources

A curated, community-maintained list of free, high-quality resources for
learning AI and improving AI literacy. Suggestions welcome via pull request.

## Beginner-friendly introductions

- **Elements of AI** — free online course by the University of Helsinki.
  https://www.elementsofai.com/
- **Google's Machine Learning Crash Course** — free, code-oriented intro.
  https://developers.google.com/machine-learning/crash-course

## Understanding large language models

- **"Illustrated Transformer"** — visual explainer of the Transformer
  architecture. https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/
- **OpenAI's API documentation** — plain-language guides for using LLMs.
  https://platform.openai.com/docs

## Critical thinking & ethics

- **MIT Stepstone** — AI literacy modules for the public.
  https://stepstone.mit.edu/
- **The Markkula Center for Applied Ethics** — AI ethics primers.
  https://www.scu.edu/ethics/ai/

## Open datasets & practice

- **Hugging Face** — open models and datasets for hands-on experimentation.
  https://huggingface.co/

> Note: This list is informational. Always review a resource's license and
> suitability before using it in your own work.
